Just a little about who I am.

Hello everybody, I want to welcome you to my website.

I'm an Artist/Designer I've been drawing since I can remember.
I have always wanted to teach drawing in away that focused on the fundamentals of drawing/designing. I like keeping the process simple, well as simple as I can.
I will be working with the basic elements and I will be using drawing principles to help focus our elements with the use of grid lines to help with scale, proportions which are principles. I will give tutorial taking you through all the steps from concept to a level where you can communicate your idea's using the elements
lines, shape and form and principles contrast, scale, proportions. I will be focusing on combining both sketching on paper, cardboard, any surface and we will be using computer to speed up the process and hopefully save lots of paper.

I am targeting beginners, people young and old,
anybody who would like to communicated their ideas with drawing and designing.
It's a wonderful and rewarding process, to take elements from nature and transform those element into something functional, stylish.
My wish is to help change how you see the world, or a least how we could see the world through drawing and designing...

I want to help you, that's my primary objective. I'm here to develop my skill also, we never really stop learning, and that's true for artist and designer too!
Imagine if there were nothing to learn...BORING!!
I hope you find my process an enjoyable one. I hate saying this but practice really does help, I will be repeating myself over and over so that the process is understood.

So get you pencils sharpened and let get scribbling!!
